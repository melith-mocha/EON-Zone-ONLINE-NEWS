Best Actress nominees Vilma Santos, Judy Ann Santos, Kathryn Bernardo and Marian Rivera

Vilma, Judy Ann, Kathryn, Marian vie for Best Actress at 41st Star Awards for Movies

By: Josh Mercado

Published: Oct 31, 2025 09:56 PM PHT

At the 41st Star Awards for Movies, which will take place at Makabagong San Juan Theater in San Juan City on Sunday, November 30, the best of Philippine cinema will once again be highlighted. It will be a night of glamour, artistic expression, and honoring exceptional contributions to the film industry.

The highly anticipated event, which is produced by GSD Studios and directed by Jorron Lee Monroy, promises to be a display of creativity, passion, and quality — honoring the timeless spirit of Filipino cinema.

The Star Awards for Movies, a four-decade tradition of grandeur and legitimacy, is an annual event held by PMPC Star Awards Inc., the country's pioneer entertainment media organization, to recognize outstanding achievements in films released in 2024.

The event, now in its 41st year, remains committed to celebrating filmmakers, performers, and industry professionals for their talent, devotion, and hard work.

For the first time, PMPC is teaming with GSD Studios (Get Stuff Done Studios), a full-service media production business that provides a variety of multi-media services, advertising, below-the-line marketing, and live event production.

“Our team is dedicated to creating high-quality, innovative content that aligns with our clients' brand goals. At GSD Studios, we pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, beyond expectations, and a determined approach,” said GSD Studios CEO Elai Tabilog.

PMPC president Mell Navarro and chairman Jimi Escala are leading this year's event, bringing new enthusiasm and vision to the legendary awards ceremony. Together with the association's dedicated members, they hope to make this event a reflection of both legacy and growth, honoring veterans while highlighting new voices and modernizing the trade.
