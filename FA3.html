Myrna Esguerra is ‘sobrang prepared po tayo’ for Miss International 2025

By: GMA Integrated News

Published: October 29, 2025 10:45am

Myrna Esguerra is set to proudly wear the Philippine sash at the upcoming Miss International 2025 pageant in Tokyo, Japan.

On Wednesday’s episode of “Unang Hirit,” the beauty queen from Abra said she took advantage of the lull between winning Binibining Pilipinas 2024 and the international competition.

“I’m not going to say that it’s a waiting time of more than one year, but it’s a preparation for more than one year,” Myrna said at her send-off event.

“Sobrang prepared po tayo — from everything, tantsado lahat,” she added.

Myrna shared she also sought advice from past Miss International Philippines delegates.

“They're all saying the same thing about the Japanese people na they are really nice,” she said. “And they also gave me advice on what I should not forget to bring with me.”

In August, the Miss International organization released the official portraits of Myrna and the other delegates.

Earlier in Missosology’s first hot picks for Miss International 2025, the Philippine bet ranked second, just behind Catalina Duque of Colombia.

Myrna will compete at the international pageant in Tokyo on November 27, where she will vie for the country's seventh Miss International crown.
